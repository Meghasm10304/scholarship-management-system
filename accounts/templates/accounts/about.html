About Us

Connecting students with scholarships worldwide

Our Mission

This platform is built with a mission to connect students with various scholarship opportunities globally. It works as a scholarship aggregator that helps students search and apply for scholarships best suited to their profiles.

The objective is to support scholarship providers such as NGOs, corporates, foundations, and individual donors by managing the end-to-end scholarship process through technology-driven, transparent, and efficient solutions.

Scholarship Facilitation Centre

The Scholarship Facilitation Centre is a one-stop platform for students where applications are forwarded to all eligible scholarship providers across India. Scholarships are matched according to the student’s profile and forwarded to the respective providers automatically, ensuring wider reach and better chances of selection.

What We Do

We connect students with scholarship providers and manage applications efficiently. Whether it’s merit-based, need-based, or study abroad, we make opportunities easier to find.
