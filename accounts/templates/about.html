About Us

This platform is built with a mission to connect students with various scholarship opportunities globally. It works as a scholarship aggregator that helps students search and apply for scholarships best suited to their profiles.

The objective is to support scholarship providers such as NGOs, corporates, foundations, and individual donors by managing the end-to-end scholarship process through technology-driven, transparent, and efficient solutions.

The platform also assists individuals and organizations in designing customized scholarship programs aligned with their social and educational vision.

Scholarship Facilitation Centre

The Scholarship Facilitation Centre is a one-stop platform for students where applications are forwarded to all eligible scholarship providers across India.

Scholarships are matched according to the student’s profile and forwarded to the respective providers automatically, ensuring wider reach and better chances of selection.

Types of Scholarships

Merit Based Scholarship

Merit scholarships are awarded to students with academic excellence, athletic, or artistic achievements. These scholarships may consider grades, leadership qualities, entrance test scores, and community involvement.

Income Based Scholarship

Income-based scholarships are awarded to students based on family income. Academic performance may also be considered, allowing financially needy yet meritorious students to access higher education.

Merit-Based (Special Talent) Scholarship

These scholarships reward academic, athletic, or artistic talent. Financial need may be considered, but the primary focus is recognizing and encouraging exceptional abilities.

Crisis Based Scholarship

Crisis-based scholarships provide financial assistance to students facing unexpected educational or economic hardships, helping them continue their studies without interruption.

Study Abroad Scholarship

Study abroad scholarships support students pursuing education overseas by covering expenses such as tuition, travel, books, and accommodation. These scholarships can be competitive and require careful application.

Loan Scholarship

Loan scholarships provide partial financial support where students repay the amount in structured installments after completing education and beginning employment.

Sports Scholarship

Sports scholarships are awarded to students with exceptional athletic abilities, enabling them to pursue education while continuing professional or competitive sports training.

Fellowship Scholarships

Fellowships are merit-based financial awards granted for advanced academic research or specialized study, typically supporting scholars pursuing higher education or research programs.
